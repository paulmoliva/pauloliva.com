Chuli Stout -
Denali Brewing Company

As a dark beer lover, I was hooked the first time I had a nitro poured Chuli Stout at Denali Brewing Company in Talkeetna. A dry Irish-style stout, it's incredibly smooth from beginning to finish. If you like dark beer, this is one you have got to try. There's nothing quite like sitting on DBC's patio on a summer day in Talkeetna, chilling out with one of these after a long day of buffoonery. Yes, please!

Peninsula Brewer's Reserve - Kenai River Brewing Company

This is a lighter beer, but there's no way you'll mistake it for Pabst Blue Ribbon. PBR is fresh and clean with a subtle hoppiness. It's crisp with a hint of fruit and finishes dry. Perfect beer for a rainy Alaska summer's day.
