Overview

The 26th Annual Southwest Alaska Economic Summit and Business Meeting will be held March 5th, 6th and 7th at the Hotel Captain Cook in Anchorage. The conference will include presentations and workshops that focus on topics important to Southwest Alaskans, such as energy planning and development, fisheries policy and sustainable fishing practices. It will also include presentations regarding the benefits of enhanced communication in Southwest Alaska, improvements in Alaska education, and an introduction to SWAMC's new business council. In addition to the presentations and workshops, SWAMC is also hosting a Conference Banquet and governor's debate. We are hoping to craft an agenda that will appeal to community and economic development interests alike, so stay tuned for more information!
