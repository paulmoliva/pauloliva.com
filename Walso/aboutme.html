Vote Myranda Walso Alaska State House District 13

Working Together for Alaska

Who am I?

My name is Myranda Walso. I am a first time candidate for public office. As a mother, entrepreneur, and lifelong Alaskan, I believe my particular skill set will be an asset to the state. I understand numbers, budgets, and financial statements. As a citizen, I emphasize the importance of financial responsibility. I have learned to listen well and communicate clearly. I know what it takes to transform complex issues into practical solutions. I think “outside the box” and offer proactive approaches with a positive attitude.

Why I'm Running

Alaska is my home, and I recognize we face trying times. Unsustainable divergence between revenue and spending levels force us to reconsider the size, structure, and role of government. The ramifications of this election will be felt most acutely by those in my generation, and our families. My children are fifth generation Alaskans, and I hope they have the same security and opportunities I enjoyed growing up. I believe wholeheartedly in the future of Alaska, but I won’t ignore the present or forget the past. The answer to the current crises cannot be found by doing more of the same.

A new solution requires new leaders. It is quite common to have representatives who have been in politics for many years, and our neighborhood is no different. I believe we need new leadership and direction. I am running because to ensure the people have a choice; the option to see a new face, with a new perspective, representing them in the Legislature.

Having never run for or held public office before, this decision was heartfelt and important to me. Involvement in local governance is essential, because the decisions made and actions taken have enormous impact on our everyday lives. I take very seriously the responsibility inherent in public service, and I want to utilize my knowledge, experience, and ability on behalf of the residents of Chugiak/Eagle River in the State Legislature and ask for your vote. I am ready for this new challenge, and with your help, on August 16, 2016 we can start making a difference together.
